Punctuation in Writing

Examples of different grammar:

  • Let's go!
  • Let's go?
  • Let's go.

Do you see how the different punctuations convey a different emotion? This is important in writing so that you can express feelings without words.

Different Tenses

There are many differrent tenses which each tell a different story.

Some examples are:

  • I'm a police officer.
  • I was a police officer.
  • She's drinking water.
  • She drinks water.
  • I'll watch that movie.
  • I watched that movie.

Vowels

In english, there are 5 vowels. There is A, E, I, O, U and depending on the situation, they are pronounced differently. For example, there is...

A has 7 pronounciations

  • Jack, Exact, Actually, Apple.
  • Yacht, Father, Alms, Barn.
  • About, Around, Zebra.
  • Football, Tall, Calling, Law.
  • Cable, Stable, Able, Ate.
  • Share, Careful, Rare, Air.
  • Private, Climate, Senate.

E has 8 pronounciations.

  • Feed, Bead.
  • Egg, Leg, Peg.
  • Herd.
  • Pretty.
  • The.
  • Cafe, They, Bay, Spray, Way, Day.
  • Gate, Mate,Late.
  • Eye.

I has 4 pronounciations.

  • Him, Tim.
  • Time, Lime.
  • King, Wing, Sing.
  • Police.

O has 3 pronounciations.

  • Above.
  • Hot, Bot, Not, Lot.
  • Boat, Moat.

U has 3 pronounciations.

  • Us, Bus, Dusty, Rusty.
  • But, Gut, Cut, Nut, Bus.
  • Fun, Gun, Sun.
