Ancient Egypt

Overview

Ancient Egypt was one of the world's oldest and most influential civilizations, located along the Nile River.

Pharaohs

Pharaohs were the rulers of ancient Egypt. Some famous ones include Ramses II and Cleopatra.

Pyramids

The pyramids, especially those at Giza, are iconic symbols of ancient Egypt.

Legacy

Ancient Egypt has had a lasting impact on art, architecture, and science worldwide.
