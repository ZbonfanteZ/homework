Punctuation in Writing

Examples of different grammar:

  • Let's go!
  • Let's go?
  • Let's go.

    • Do you see how the different punctuations convey a different emotion? This is important in writing so that you can express feelings without words.

    Different Tenses

    There are many differrent tenses which each tell a different story.

Some examples are:

  • I'm a police officer.
  • I was a police officer.
  • She's drinking water.
  • She drinks water.
  • I'll watch that movie.
  • I watched that movie.
